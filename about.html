About EWU Matrimony

Building meaningful connections within the EWU community

Our Story

Why We Started

EWU Matrimony was born from a simple idea: that the best life partners are often found within communities that share common values, education, and experiences. As EWU alumni ourselves, we believe our university community provides the perfect foundation for meaningful, lasting relationships.

Our Mission

To create a safe, trusted platform where EWU students and alumni can find their perfect life partners within our university community.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted matrimony platform for EWU graduates, helping build strong families rooted in shared educational values.

Our Values

Trust, privacy, respect, and authenticity are at the core of everything we do for our EWU family.
