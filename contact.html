FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join EWU Matrimony?

Only current students and alumni of East West University with a valid @ewubd.edu email can create an account.

How is my profile verified?

We send a verification link to your EWU email address. You must click this link to verify your account.

Is EWU Matrimony free to use?

Basic features are free. Premium features like unlimited messaging and priority visibility require a subscription.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the settings page, or contact our support team for assistance.
